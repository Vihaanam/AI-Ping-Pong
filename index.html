AI Ping Pong




Originally made by Prashant Shukla, edited by Vihaan

Instructions

  1. First keep your device screen straight.
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet from the device.
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner. A red dot should appear.
  4. Now press the play button and move yourself 3-4 feet from the device again.
  5. The red paddel is your paddel which will move with the movement of your wrist.
